About

The Kendo Club at UCSD was founded in 1998 and remains active to this day. Our club seeks to promote awareness and appreciation of the Japanese martial art, kendo (剣道). Our members have gone on to compete at the national and international level.

The Kendo Recreation Class is offered through the UCSD Recreation Department. It’s a great way for beginners to learn kendo from an experienced individual in a welcoming environment!
